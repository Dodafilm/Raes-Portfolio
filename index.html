About

Photo

Creating at the intersection of design & fine art.

Rae Holt is a multidisciplinary designer and artist whose work bridges the gap between commercial design and fine art. With a keen eye for color, composition, and storytelling, Rae brings a painterly sensibility to every project — whether it's a brand campaign, album cover, or canvas painting.

With experience spanning marketing design, social media content creation, album artwork, and commercial branding, Rae's versatile practice is rooted in a deep love for visual expression. Each piece is crafted with intention, balancing aesthetic beauty with strategic purpose.

When not designing for clients, Rae can be found in the studio, exploring color and texture through painting — a practice that continually informs and enriches the commercial work.
