About

Creating at the intersection of design & fine art.

Hi there! I'm Rachele Holt, a graphic designer with 5+ years of experience creating brand-forward, conversion-driven visuals across digital, print, and social. Known for clean design systems, storytelling with purpose, and fast-turnaround execution.

With a BFA from SUNY Plattsburgh and experience spanning freelance design, healthcare marketing, and retail project management, I bring a painterly sensibility to every project — whether it's a brand campaign, album cover, or canvas painting.

Outside of work, I find joy in painting, attending live music events, and experimenting with new recipes. I believe in infusing creativity into every aspect of life to bring a fresh perspective to both my work and personal pursuits.
